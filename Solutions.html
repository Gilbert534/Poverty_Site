Solutions for rural Poverty



  1. Make agriculture more inclusive, sustainable and productive. Some people in the country side depend on agriculture for their survival and to helping out their family, so making agriculture more inclusive can help close the poverty gap.

  2. Land reform is another way to help the rural poor in the world. With land reform, access to land is what divides the rich rural people and the poor rural people. Access to land gives families a reliable and productive way to make an income.

  3. Creating more and better jobs will allow people of different ages obtain a better income for their families, especially the young people.
